Our Mission

Our main mission is to make the transition from a sedentary lifestyle to fitness lifestyle seamless. Going to a gym for the very first time can be extremly intimidating. Many individuals stop working out due to feeling embarrassed and intimidated as well as feeling like the entire gym is looking at them. That stops today at Mike's Gym. Our staff will answer all questions about particular workouts and how to operate gym equipment without feeling embarrassed. All your needs will be covered and we will help to acheive your goals.
